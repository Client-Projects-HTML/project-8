Our Mission

Delivering Quality Roofing Solutions with Integrity

At RoofPro, our mission is simple: provide exceptional commercial roofing services that protect businesses and their assets while exceeding expectations on every project. We believe in transparency, quality craftsmanship, and building lasting relationships with our clients.

Founded in 2004, we've grown from a small local contractor to a regional leader in commercial roofing, serving hundreds of businesses across the country. Our success is built on our commitment to excellence and our dedication to customer satisfaction.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to industry leaders

2004

The Beginning

Founded by John Smith with a vision to provide quality commercial roofing services. Started with a team of 5 dedicated professionals.

2008

Regional Expansion

Expanded operations to serve the entire state. Added specialized services including TPO and EPDM installations.

2014

Technology Investment

Introduced drone inspection technology and advanced roofing techniques. Became industry leader in innovation.

2024

Today

Over 2,500 projects completed, 50+ expert roofers, serving businesses nationwide with comprehensive roofing solutions.
